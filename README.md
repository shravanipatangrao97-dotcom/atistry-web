## ✨ Overview

Atistry is a modern art portfolio and gallery experience designed for independent artists who want more than just a website.

The goal of this project was to create a digital environment that feels as expressive as the artwork itself—blending immersive design, interactive galleries, and thoughtful storytelling to showcase an artist's work in a memorable way.

What started as a conversation with a friend about her artwork evolved into a full-scale design and development project focused on helping artists present and sell their work online.

---

## 🎯 Project Vision

Traditional portfolio websites often display artwork as static images in a grid.

Atistry explores a different approach:

* 🖼️ Art-first storytelling
* 🎨 Editorial-inspired layouts
* 🌿 Soft, handcrafted visual aesthetics
* ✨ Interactive gallery experiences
* 📱 Mobile-first premium user experience
* 🚀 Future-ready e-commerce architecture

The objective is not simply to showcase paintings, but to create a digital experience that reflects the personality of the artist.

## 🎨 Design Philosophy

The design language draws inspiration from:

* Physical art galleries
* Scrapbooks and journals
* Editorial layouts
* Museum exhibitions
* Contemporary artist portfolios

Every section was designed with the intention of making the visitor feel like they are stepping into the artist's world rather than browsing a traditional website.
